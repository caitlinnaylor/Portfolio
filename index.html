NZ Agrifood Week Agtech Hackathon Winner
Palmerston North Mayors' Innovation Award

Over around 10 hours, two friends and I competed in an AgTech Hackathon against people of all ages and experiences.

Our pitched idea of AI Goggles to help harvesters identify disease in fruit managed to win the competition, with the judges particularly noting the quality of our presentation.

We also gained the attention of Zespri who later visited our school to meet us and the Mayor Grant Smith, whose award we had also won.

Royal Society Gold Team CREST

Over 18 months, my friend and I created a HTML/CSS/JavaScript template for a school navigational website that can be easily adjusted to fit various schools and completed the multiple reports and presentations required to gain this award.

Read more about the project here or check out the Royal Society website.
